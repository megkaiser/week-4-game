CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the displayed random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the displayed random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


The displayed random number and the value of each crystal changes with the start of each game.
